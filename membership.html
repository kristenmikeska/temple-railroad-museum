NARM Reciprocal Benefits

Family-level members and above gain reciprocal access to hundreds of museums across the United States, Canada, and beyond through the North American Reciprocal Museum (NARM) Association.

One Temple Railroad membership can open doors at science centers, art museums, children's museums, botanical gardens, and more — wherever you travel. It's one of the most overlooked benefits of membership.

Questions

Membership FAQ

When does my membership start?

Current memberships run May 2025 through April 2026. New memberships purchased mid-cycle begin immediately and run through April 2026.

Can I give a membership as a gift?

Absolutely. Gift memberships make wonderful presents for history lovers, families with children, and anyone who loves trains. Contact us to set one up.

What is NARM access?

NARM (North American Reciprocal Museum) is a network of hundreds of museums that offer free or discounted admission to members of participating institutions. Family-level and above are included.

How do I use my caboose rental (Superintendent)?

Contact the museum at 254-298-5172 to schedule your complimentary 3-hour caboose rental. Subject to availability. Great for private events and photos.

Housed in the historic 1911 Santa Fe Depot, the museum preserves the railroad heritage and community history of Temple, Texas and all of Central Texas.
