Group Tours & School Visits

Bring your class, your organization, or your club to the 1911 Santa Fe Depot for a guided experience tailored to your group's interests and grade level.

Tour Options

Experiences for Every Group

🏫

School Field Trips

Curriculum-aligned tours designed for K–12 students, connecting Texas history standards to hands-on exploration of the depot, exhibits, and rolling stock.

  • Grades K–12
  • Guided by museum educators
  • Interactive stations included
  • Pre- and post-visit resources available
  • Discounted group admission
🏢

Corporate & Organization Tours

A unique team-building or networking experience in one of Temple's most distinctive historic spaces. Great for onboarding, client events, or community outreach.

  • Groups of any size
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Add a private reception option
  • Catering coordination available
👴

Senior & Community Groups

Accessible, engaging tours for senior centers, civic organizations, and community groups — perfect for anyone who remembers when the railroad was still the backbone of Central Texas.

  • ADA accessible throughout
  • Relaxed pace tours available
  • Group discounted admission
  • Seating available throughout depot

Book a Tour

Let's Plan Your Visit

Group tours must be scheduled in advance. We recommend booking at least two weeks ahead, especially for school groups during the fall and spring semesters.

  • Minimum group size: 10 people
  • Available Tuesday through Friday
  • Morning and afternoon slots
  • Discounted admission for groups of 15+
  • Free chaperone admission for school groups
  • Parking available on-site at no charge

Request a Group Tour

We'll follow up within 2 business days.
