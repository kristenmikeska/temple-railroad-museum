Before You Book

Rental FAQ

What's included in the rental fee?

All rentals include access to the space, on-site parking, tables and chairs (quantities vary by space), and coordination with our facility manager. Catering and A/V are arranged separately.

Can we have catering?

Yes — you are welcome to bring your own approved caterer or use one of our preferred vendor partners. A kitchen prep area is available for catering use.

Are alcohol events permitted?

Alcohol is permitted with a TABC-licensed bar service and the appropriate insurance. Our rental coordinator can walk you through the requirements.

Is the depot accessible?

Yes. The depot is fully ADA accessible with ramp entry, elevator access between floors, and accessible restrooms throughout.
