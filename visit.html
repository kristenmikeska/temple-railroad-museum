Virtual Experience

Explore the Depot in 3D

Can't make it to Temple in person? Our immersive Matterport 3D virtual tour lets you step inside the historic 1911 Santa Fe Depot from anywhere in the world.

Walk the second-floor galleries, examine the permanent collection, explore the rolling stock on the grounds, and get a feel for the scale and grandeur of the restored depot — all from your screen.

Accessibility & Amenities

We Welcome Everyone

♿

Accessible Entrance

The depot is fully ADA accessible with ramp entry, elevators, and accessible restrooms on both floors.

🎒

Family Friendly

Interactive stations throughout the museum are designed for children. Strollers are welcome inside the galleries.

🛍️

Gift Shop

Members receive 15–20% off all gift shop purchases. Find books, prints, branded items, and unique railroad keepsakes.

📸

Photography

Personal photography is welcome throughout the museum and grounds. Please ask staff about photography for commercial use.

Housed in the historic 1911 Santa Fe Depot, the museum preserves the railroad heritage and community history of Temple, Texas and all of Central Texas.
