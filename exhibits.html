On the Grounds

Historic Rolling Stock

The museum grounds display an impressive collection of historic rolling stock — the actual trains and rail cars that once moved across Texas. Walk alongside a Santa Fe caboose, a diesel locomotive, sleeper cars, and boxcars, all adjacent to an active BNSF and Amtrak rail yard.

On any given visit, you may hear or see a modern freight or passenger train passing — the same view Temple's earliest residents had in 1881.
